LOCATION

The Depot food truck park is in the epicenter of the tourist triangle of Santa Cruz.

Nestled between Downtown, the Boardwalk, and the Warf, The Depot is the perfect place to grab a bite and rest between attractions.

Get to know these popular Santa Cruz locations by using the links bellow:

santa cruz beach boardwalk

The historic Boardwalk is one of the most popular places to visit. An abundance of rides, games, and warm california sun are here for everyone to enjoy, not to mention the beach!



downtown

Pacific Avenue is host to shops, restuarants, and bars. Many shops take pride in supporting local emerging artists and creatives. Come down on a Wednesday and enjoy the farmers market!



The Warf

The home of Marini's Candies where you can pick up a bag of authentic salt water taffy. Enjoy browsing the fresh fish markets or go watch the harbor seals who live under the warf.
